Fifteen Puzzle Tutorial

How to Play the Fifteen Puzzle

Goal: Arrange the 15 numbered tiles in numerical order from left to right, top to bottom, with the empty space in the bottom right.

Movement: Slide tiles horizontally or vertically to move them into the open position.

Starting Position: The tiles are randomly placed in the grid before play begins.

Tips: Focus on solving one row or column at a time to gradually bring the tiles into order.
